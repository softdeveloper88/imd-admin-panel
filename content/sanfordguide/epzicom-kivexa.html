Epzicom, Kivexa
Tradename: Abacavir + Lamivudine

Usage and Dosing

  • Indicated for use in ART as a dual nucleoside fixed-dose combination component of a 3-drug regimen; usually paired with a NNRTI, a boosted PI, or an INSTI.
  • Black Box warnings: abacavir hypersensitivity (severe reactions may be somewhat more frequent with 600 mg dose), exacerbation of hepatitis B following discontinuation of 3TC.
  • Test HLA-B*5701 before use.
  • Do not use in individuals with Chronic Hepatitis B infection (without entecavir); cannot use with TDF or TAF owing to drug-drug interaction with abacavir (reduced antiretroviral effect).
  • 2024 IAS-USA recommendation: Patients with, or at high risk for, cardiovascular disease who are receiving an abacavir-containing regimen should switch to a non-abacavir-containing regimen if an active regimen is available (JAMA 2024 Dec 1. doi: 10.1001/jama.2024.24543 [online ahead of print].
  • See individual components for drug-specific data:
    • Abacavir (ABC)
    • Lamivudine (3TC)
  • For class-wide adverse effects, see ARV Drugs, Overview.
  • Available in a generic formulation.

      Adverse Effects

      • Hypersensitivity reactions.
      • Lactic acidosis, severe hepatomegaly with steatosis.
      • Immune reconstitution syndrome (IRIS).
      • See individual components for more information.
