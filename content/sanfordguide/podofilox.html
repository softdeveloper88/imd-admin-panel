Podofilox
Tradename: Condylox

Usage and Dosing

  • Podofilox is available as a 0.5% topical gel or solution for treatment of warts: external genital and/or perianal condylomata acuminata only.

Adult Dose

  • 0.5% gel or solution 2x daily for 3 days, then no therapy for 4 days (can repeat up to 4 such cycles)

Pediatric Dose

Dose (Age >28 Days) Safety and efficacy not established

Adverse Effects

  • Local reactions—pain, burning, inflammation in 50%. Can ulcerate. Limit surface area treated as per label.
