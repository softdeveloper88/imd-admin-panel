Harvoni: Ledipasvir + Sofosbuvir
Tradename: Harvoni: Ledipasvir + Sofosbuvir

Usage and Dosing

  • Single tablet fixed-dose combination of Ledipasvir, a NS5A inhibitor, + Sofosbuvir, a NS5B RNA polymerase inhibitor, for treatment of hepatitis C virus (HCV).
  • First agent for treatment of genotype 1 HCV without Ribavirin and Interferon.
  • Test for evidence of current or prior Hepatitis B (HBV) infection: measure HBsAG and anti-HBC before initiating HCV treatment. If HBV co-infection, monitor patient for HBV reactivation during and post-HCV therapy.
  • Product availability:
    • Tablets (Ledipasvir/Sofosbuvir): 90 mg/400 mg, 45 mg/200 mg
    • Oral pellets (Ledipasvir/Sofosbuvir): 45 mg/200 mg, 33.75 mg/150 mg
    • Launch plans for the oral pellets and the 45 mg/200 mg tablet strength are pending as of August 2019.
  • Caution: numerous drug interactions (see Table below, not all-inclusive).

      Adverse Effects

      • Most common: fatigue (16%), headache (14%), nausea (7%), diarrhea (3%), insomnia (5%)
      • Black Box Warning: Cases of HBV reactivation, occasionally fulminant, during or after DAA therapy have been reported in HBV/HCV coninfected patients who were not already on HBV suppressive therapy.
