Penicillins, Overview

Introduction

  • The drugs that comprise the penicillin category of antibiotics are listed below.
    • The penicillin chemical structure includes a beta-lactam ring, a thiazolidine ring, and an R-1 side chain.
    • The other categories of antibiotics that contain a beta-lactam ring are cephalosporins, carbapenems, and the monobactam (aztreonam).
