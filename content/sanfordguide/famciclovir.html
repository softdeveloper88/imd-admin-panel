Famciclovir
Tradename: Famvir

Usage and Dosing

  • Famciclovir is a prodrug. It is metabolized to the active antiviral compound, penciclovir.
  • Used for treatment of herpesvirus infections: herpes simplex (HSV) and varicella zoster (VZV), but not cytomegalovirus (CMV).

Adult Dose

Herpes labialis (cold sores)1500 mg po x1 dose
Primary genital herpes (HIV, non-HIV)250 mg po tid x7-10 days
Recurrent genital herpes (non-HIV)1000 mg po bid x1 day, or 125 mg po bid x5 days
Recurrent orolabial, genital herpes (HIV)500 mg po bid x7 days
Chronic suppression of genital herpes250 mg po bid
Herpes zoster (Shingles)(non-HIV)500 mg po tid x7 days

Adverse Effects

  • Similar to Acyclovir, included headache, nausea, diarrhea, and dizziness but incidence does not differ from placebo.

Pregnancy Risk

FDA Risk Category 1 No evidence of toxicity in humans or animals
