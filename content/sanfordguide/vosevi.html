Vosevi: Sofosbuvir + Velpatasvir + Voxilaprevir
Tradename: Vosevi: Sofosbuvir + Velpatasvir + Voxilaprevir

Usage and Dosing

  • Vosevi is a fixed dose combination (FDC) of Sofosbuvir (NS5B) + Velpatasvir (NS5A)  + Voxilaprevir (NS3/4A Protease).
  • The dosage in each FDC tablet consists of: 400 mg of sofosbuvir + 100 mg of velpatasvir + 100 mg of voxilaprevir
  • Caution: numerous drug interactions (see chart below, not all-inclusive)

  • The FDC is indicated for the treatment of adult patients with chronic HCV infection without cirrhosis or with compensated cirrhosis (Child-Pugh A) who have genotype 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6 infection and have previously been treated with an HCV regimen containing an NS5A inhibitor and for treatment of genotype 1a or 3 infection and have previously been treated with an HCV regimen containing sofosbuvir without an NS5A inhibitor.

      Adverse Effects

      • Most common:  headache, fatigue, diarrhea, and nausea 
      • Black Box Warning: Use with caution in those patients with concomitant Hepatitis B infection, especially if the Hep B viral infection is not being treated. Watch for flare of HBV infection, associated with spike in liver transaminases, which may be fatal.
