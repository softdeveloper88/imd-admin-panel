Editorial Note to Readers

  • This section on diagnosis and differential diagnosis of AIDS-related clinical syndromes, opportunistic infections and neoplasms (drawn from Table 11 in the former print edition of the Sanford Guide to HIV/AIDS Therapy) had its genesis in the early days of the AIDS epidemic before the advent of potent antiretroviral therapy and the current generation of antiretroviral drugs and rapid diagnostic tests. As a result, some of the material is less relevant in clinical practice than before, but it nonetheless remains an important part of our overall coverage of HIV/AIDS.
