Ansuvimab-zykl
Tradename: Ebanga

Usage and Dosing

  • Ansuvimab-zykl (Ebanga) is a glycoprotein-directed human monoclonal antibody FDA-approved for treatment of Zaire ebolavirus in adults and children, including neonates.
  • Efficacy has not been established for other Ebola virus species or for Marburg virus species.

Adult Dose

  • 50 mg/kg IV single infusion (diluted in NS or LR) over 60 min.
  • Steps for reconstitution:
    • Allow vials to reach ambient temperature (15-27°C) for approximately 20 minutes.
    • Dilute with 7.7 mL of sterile water. Inject diluent along with vial wall to avoid foaming.
    • Gently swirl vial (do not shake) for 10 seconds, pause for 10 seconds, and repeat until the cake is dissolved.
    • This produces 8 mL of solution (50 mg/mL).
  • Dilute in NS or LR according to the following chart (ensures a final concentration of 8-30 mg/mL) and infuse with a 1.2 micron filter via a central line or peripheral catheter. Use within four hours of preparation.
