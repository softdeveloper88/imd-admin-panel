Mastitis, Overview

Overview

  • Staphylococcus aureus is the most common cause of mastitis.
  • Cultures of expressed breast milk should be obtained in more severe cases, e.g., in those not responding to empirical antibiotics after two days of therapy; and, if MRSA is a consideration.
  • For a review with definitions, see Am Fam Physician 78:727, 2008.and StatPearls; 2021 Jan – 2021 Mar 12.

Specific Topics

  • Postpartum mastitis
  • Non-puerperal mastitis
  • Idiopathic granulomatous mastitis
  • Breast implant infection
