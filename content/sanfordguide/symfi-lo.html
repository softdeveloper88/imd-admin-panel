Symfi, Symfi Lo
Tradename: Efavirenz + Lamivudine + Tenofovir disproxil

Usage and Dosing

  • Symfi and Symfi Lo are single tablet, fixed dose combination regimens for use in adults and children (weight ≥40 kg and ≥35 kg, respectively) with HIV-1 infection.
  • Formulation:
    • Efavirenz (EFV)
    • Lamivudine (3TC)
    • Tenofovir disoproxil fumarate (TDF)
  • Symfi is a generic version of Atripla (substituting 3TC for FTC). In Symfi Lo, the dose of EFV is lower (400 mg instead of 600 mg), which is associated with lower toxicity, better tolerability, and similar efficacy as the brand name formulation.
  • Caution: numerous drug interactions. See table below (not all inclusive).

Adult Dose

  • Usual dose of Symfi and Symfi Lo: one tablet once daily, typically at night (HS)
  • Symfi: EFV 600 mg + 3TC 300 mg + TDF 300 mg
  • Symfi Lo: EFV 400 mg + 3TC 300 mg + TDF 300 mg
