Sinecatechins
Tradename: Veregen

Usage and Dosing

  • Sinecatechins is a botanical product available as a 15% ointment. 
  • Extract from Green Tea
  • Sinecatechins are mostly catechins, 55% of which is epigallocatechin gallate.
  • First botanical drug approved by the US FDA
  • Warts: external genital and/or perianal condylomata acuminata only; less efficacious than podophyllotoxin 0·5% or imiquimod 5% (Reviewed BMJ, 2019)

Adult Dose

  • 15% ointment. Apply 0.5 cm strand to each wart 3x/day
    until healing but not more than 16 weeks.

Pediatric Dose

Dose (Age >28 Days) Safety and efficacy not established

Adverse Effects

  • Application site reactions, which may result in ulcerations, phimosis, meatal stenosis, superinfection.
