Lactobacillus sp.

Clinical Setting

  • Positive blood culture in patient with an IV line.
  • Immunocompromised hosts.
  • Specific therapy.
  • Removal of infected IV line is primary therapy.

Classification

  • Gram positive bacilli, microaerophilic

Primary Regimens

  • Penicillin G 2 million units IV q4h
  • Ampicillin 2 gm IV q4h

Alternative Regimens

  • Clindamycin 600 mg IV q8h

Comments

  • May be resistant to Vancomycin.
  • Combination often used, at least initially; see Clin Infect Dis 38: 62, 2004.
  • For species-specific susceptibility to various antimicrobials see Clin Infect Dis 42: e35, 2006.
