Arthritis, Septic, Post Intra-articular Injection, Post-arthroscopy

Clinical Setting

  • Septic joint infection following intra-articular injections.

Etiologies

  • S. epidermidis (MSSE/MRSE) (40%)
  • Staph. aureus (MSSA/MRSA) (20%)
  • P. aeruginosa
  • Cutibacterium acnes (formerly Propionibacterium acnes)
  • Mycobacteria sp.
  • Fungi

Primary Regimens

  • See Septic Arthritis, Adult or Septic Arthritis, Child or Septic Arthritis, Infant

Alternative Regimens

  • Not applicable.

Comments

  • None
