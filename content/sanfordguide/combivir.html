Combivir
Tradename: Combivir

Usage and Dosing

  • Indicated for use as a fixed-dose dual nucleoside as part of a 3-drug combination regimen, usually paired with a NNRTI, a boosted PI, or a STII (Strand-transfer Integrase Inhibitor)
  • Black Box warning—exacerbation of Hepatitis B in patients stopping 3TC.
  • See individual components for drug-specific data:
    • Lamivudine (3TC)
    • Zidovudine (ZDV)
  • For class-wide adverse effects, see ARV Drugs, Overview.
  • Both drugs are available in generic formulations
