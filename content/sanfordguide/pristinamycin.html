Pristinamycin
Tradename: Pyostacine

Usage and Dosing

  • Pristinamycin is a mixture of pristinamycin IA and pristinamycin IIA.
  • Pristinamycin IA (PIA) is a group B streptogramin, pristinamycin IIA (PIIA) is a group A streptogramin.
  • Group A and B streptogramins bind to the 50S subunit of the bacterial ribosome. Individually they are bacteriostatic, but in combination may be bactericidal.
  • Available in Europe (France, Belgium), but not marketed in the U.S.

Adult Dose

  • 2-4 gm/day (divided q8-12h).

Pediatric Dose

Dose (Age >28 Days) 50-100 mg/kg/day (divided q8h)

Adverse Effects

  • GI (nausea, vomiting)
  • Hypersensitivity reactions (uncommon)
  • Pseudomembranous colitis
