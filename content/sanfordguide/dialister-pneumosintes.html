Dialister pneumosintes

Clinical Setting

  • Positive lab result
  • Oral flora associated with periodontal and other oral infections. Rarely intra-abdominal and soft tissue abscesses.

Classification

  • Anaerobic gram-negative rod

Primary Regimens

  • Amoxicillin or Piperacillin

Alternative Regimens

  • Metronidazole
  • Carbapenems

Comments

  • Limited data on susceptibility: see Antimicrob Agents Chemother 2007; 51:4498
