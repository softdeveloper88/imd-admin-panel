Bite: Komodo Dragon

Clinical Setting

  • Bite wound from Komodo dragon (Varanus komodoensis)

Etiologies

  • Staphylococcus spp
  • Bacillus spp
  • Aeromonas spp
  • Pseudomonas spp
  • Enterobacteriaceae
  • Burkholderia
  • Anaerobes

Primary Regimens

  • (Amoxicillin-clavulanate 875/125 + Ciprofloxacin 750 mg) po bid

Alternative Regimens

  • Piperacillin-tazobactam 4.5 gm IV (over 4 hr) q8h

Comments

  • Administer tetanus toxoid or Tdap (preferred) if > 10 years since last dose
  • Microbiology: see Clin Micro Rev 24: 231, 2011.
  • Also have toxic venom Proc Natl Acad Sci 106: 8969, 2009.
  • see Clinical Infectious Diseases 2014;59(2):e10–5.
