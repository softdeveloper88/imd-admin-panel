Bite: Shark

Clinical Setting

  • Bite of shark, in saltwater or occasionally brackish water
  • Obtain cultures
  • Manage crush injury and trauma (Am Surg 76:101 2010)

Etiologies

  • Gram negative organisms (60-80% of flora)
    • Vibrio spp.
    • Pasteurella spp.
    • Aeromonas spp.
    • Klebsiella spp
    • Pseudomonas spp
  • Gram positive organisms
    • Staph spp
    • Clostridia perfringens and other Clostridia spp.
    • Enterococcus spp.

Primary Regimens

  • Ceftriaxone + Doxycycline

Alternative Regimens

  • Piperacillin-tazobactam
  • Meropenem
