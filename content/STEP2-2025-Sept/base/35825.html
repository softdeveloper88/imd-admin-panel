Eating disorders

Diagnosis

Clinical features

Treatment

Anorexia nervosa
  • BMI <18.5 kg/m2
  • Intense fear of weight gain
  • Distorted views of body weight & shape
  • Cognitive-behavioral therapy
  • Nutritional rehabilitation
  • Olanzapine if no response to above

Bulimia nervosa
  • Recurrent episodes of binge eating
  • Binge eating & inappropriate compensatory behavior to prevent weight gain
  • Excess worrying about body shape & weight
  • Cognitive-behavioral therapy
  • Nutritional rehabilitation
  • SSRI (fluoxetine), often in combination with above

Binge-eating disorder
  • Recurrent episodes of binge eating
  • No inappropriate compensatory behaviors
  • Lack of control during eating
  • Cognitive-behavioral therapy
  • Behavioral weight loss therapy
  • SSRI
  • Lisdexamfetamine

SSRI = selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor.
