HCV Drugs, Overview

 

HCV Direct-acting Agents, Overview

  • Direct-acting agents target several regions within the HCV genome:
    • NS3-4a: HCV Serine Protease gene (NS3); Serine Protease Co-factor (NS4a)
    • NS5a: Inhibition of viral particle assembly
    • NS5b: RNA-Dependent/RNA Polymerase (Nucleosides and Non-Nucleosides)
  • Some drugs enhance activity of Interferon (e.g., cyclophilin inhibitors).
