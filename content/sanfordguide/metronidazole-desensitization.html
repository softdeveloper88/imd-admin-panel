Metronidazole Desensitization

Overview

  • Protocol for oral desensitization to Metronidazole.
  • Reference: Allergy Rhinol 5:1, 2014

Method

  • Stop after the 500 mg dose if prescribing 500 mg po bid x7 days.
  • Complete entire protocol if prescribing 2 gm po x1.
  • The authors recommend full strength skin-prick (2 mg/mL) and intradermal testing (1:10,000, 1:1000, and 1:100) before desensitization.
