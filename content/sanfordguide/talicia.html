Talicia
Tradename: Talicia

Usage and Dosing

  • Three drug combination of omeprazole magnesium + amoxicillin + rifabutin for treatment of Helicobacter pylori infection in adults.
  • Each capsule contains:
    • Omeprazole (10 mg)
    • Amoxicillin (250 mg)
    • Rifabutin (12.5 mg)
  • Omeprazole is included in the delayed-release component of the capsule, amoxicillin and rifabutin in the immediate-release component.
  • Caution: numerous drug interactions (see chart below, not all inclusive).

Adult Dose

  • 4 capsules q8h with food x14 days
    • Swallow whole. Do not crush or chew.
    • Do not take with alcohol.

Adverse Effects

  • Warnings
    • C. difficile-associated diarrhea (CDAD)
    • Reduced efficacy of hormonal contraceptives
    • Acute tubulointerstitial nephritis
    • Cutaneous and systemic lupus erythematosus
    • Rash in patients with mononucleosis
    • Uveitis
    • Interactions with diagnostic evaluations for neuroendocrine tumors
  • Adverse reactions
    • GI (nausea/vomiting/diarrhea)
    • Headache
    • Abdominal pain
    • Chromaturia
    • Hypersensitivity reactions
    • Dyspepsia
    • Oropharyngeal pain
    • Vulvovaginal candidiasis
