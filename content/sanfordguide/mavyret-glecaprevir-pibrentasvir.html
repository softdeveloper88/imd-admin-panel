Mavyret: Glecaprevir + Pibrentasvir
Tradename: Mavyret: Glecaprevir + Pibrentasvir

Usage and Dosing

  • Mavyret is a fixed-dose combination of glecaprevir, an HCV NS3/4A protease inhibitor (PI), and pibrentasvir, an HCV NS5A inhibitor. It is a pangenotypic regimen.
  • Available formulations:
    • Tablets containing 100 mg glecaprevir + 40 mg pibrentasvir.
    • Packets of oral pellets containing 50 mg glecaprevir + 20 mg pibrentasvir.
  • Indicated for the treatment of adult and pediatric patients ≥3 years of age with chronic HCV genotype 1-6 infection without cirrhosis or with compensated cirrhosis (Child-Pugh A).
  • Also indicated for the treatment of adult and pediatric patients ≥3 years of age with HCV genotype 1 infection who previously have been treated with a regimen containing an HCV NS5A inhibitor or an NS3/4A protease inhibitor (PI), but not both.
  • Contraindicated in persons with moderate or severe hepatic impairment (Child-Pugh B or C) or those with any history of prior hepatic decompensation.
  • Black Box Warning: risk of hepatitis B virus reactivation in patients co-infected with HCV and HBV. Test all patients for evidence of current or prior HBV infection before initiating therapy.
  • Caution: numerous drug interactions (see Major Interactions table below, not all-inclusive).
  • Recommended treatment duration in patients ≥3 years of age:

Adult Dose

  • Three (3) tablets once daily with food.
  • Total daily dose: glecaprevir 300 mg, pibrentasvir 120 mg.
  • Each tablet contains 100 mg glecaprevir + 40 mg pibrentasvir.

      Adverse Effects

      • Most common: headache, fatigue
      • GI: nausea, diarrhea
      • Black Box Warning: risk of hepatitis B virus reactivation in patients co-infected with HCV and HBV.
