Iodoquinol
Tradename: Yodoxin

Usage and Dosing

  • Acts in lumen of GI tract on treatment for amebiasis, balantidiasis, and infections due to D. fragilis.
  • See Antiparasitic Drugs, Sources. Sometimes available from Expert Compounding Pharmacy

Adult Dose

IndicationDose
Asymptomatic cyst passage or active diarrhea due to E. histolytica:650 mg po after meals tid x 20 days
Alternative agent for Balantiodes coli:650 mg po after meals tid x 20 days
Primary therapy for Dientamoeba fragilis:650 mg po after meals tid x 20 days
Alternative agent for Blastocystis hominis:650 mg po after meals tid x 20 days

Adverse Effects

  • CNS AEs reported: fever, agitation, retrograde amnesia
  • Iodine hypersensitivity rash (Iododerma); thyroid gland enlargement.
  • Contraindicated if iodine intolerance or hepatic damage.
  • Optic neuritis and peripheral neuropathy can occur with protracted high dose therapy.

Antimicrobial Spectrum

  • Balantidium coli
  • Blastocystis hominis
  • Dientamoeba histolytica
  • Entamoeba histolytica
