Odefsey
Tradename: Odefsey: Emtricitabine + Rilpivirine + Tenofovir alafenamide fumarate

Usage and Dosing

  • Odefsey is a once-daily, fixed-dose 3-drug combination regimen for patients with HIV-1 infection age 12 years and older.
    • ART-naive patients (HIV-1 RNA ≤ 100,000 copies/mL)
    • Replacement regimen for virologically-suppressed patients (HIV-1 RNA < 50 copies/mL for ≥ 6 months)
  • Before starting: test for Hepatitis B (HBV) infection and obtain estimated CrCl (not recommended for CrCl < 30 mL/min), urine glucose and urine protein.
  • Monitor HIV-1 RNA to assess virologic failure / rebound.
  • See individual components for drug-specific data:
    • Rilpivirine (RPV)
    • Emtricitabine (FTC)
    • Tenofovir alafenamide fumarate (TAF)
  • For class-wide adverse effects, see ARV Drugs, Overview.
  • Caution: numerous drug interactions (see below)

Adult Dose

  • 1 tablet po q24h with food
  • Tablets: RPV 25 mg + FTC 200 mg + TAF 25 mg
