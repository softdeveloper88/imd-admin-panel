Zepatier: Elbasvir + Grazoprevir
Tradename: Zepatier: Elbasvir + Grazoprevir

Usage and Dosing

  • Zepatier (Elbasvir + Grazoprevir) is a fixed dose combination of NS5A inhibitor (Elbasvir) and NS3/4A inhibitor (Grazoprevir) indicated with or without Ribavirin for treatment of chronic Hepatitis C (HCV) genotypes 1 or 4 infection in adults.
  • Prior to initiation of therapy, testing for presence of NS5A resistance is recommended.
  • Test for evidence of current or prior Hepatitis B (HBV) infection: measure HBsAG and anti-HBC before initiating HCV treatment. If HBV co-infection, monitor patient for HBV reactivation during and post-HCV therapy.
  • Caution: numerous drug interactions and contraindications (see chart below, not all-inclusive)
  • Duration of treatment ranges from 12-16 weeks:

Adult Dose

  • Usual Dose: One tablet (elbasvir 50 mg + grazoprevir 100 mg) po q24h with or without food

    Adverse Effects

    • Most common:
      • Fatigue, headache, nausea
      • If RBV added: anemia, headache
    • Black Box Warning: Cases of HBV reactivation, occasionally fulminant, during or after DAA therapy have been reported in HBV/HCV coninfected patients who were not already on HBV suppressive therapy.
