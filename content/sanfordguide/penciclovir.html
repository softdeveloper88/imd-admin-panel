Penciclovir
Tradename: Denavir, Vectavir, Fenivir

Usage and Dosing

  • Penciclovir is poorly absorbed orally; used as a topical preparation.
  • Apply topical Penciclovir to area of recurrence of herpes labialis with start of symptoms. Well-tolerated.
  • Famciclovir is a pro-drug of Penciclovir and has better bioavailability.

Adult Dose

Penciclovir 1% creamApply q2h (while awake) x4 days

Pediatric Dose

Dose (Age >28 Days) Age ≥12 yr: Apply q2h (while awake) x4 days
