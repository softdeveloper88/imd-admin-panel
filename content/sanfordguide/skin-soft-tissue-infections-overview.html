Skin & Soft Tissue Infections, Overview

Overview

  • Infections of the skin (epidermis), skin structures and soft tissue (SSTI) cover a wide range of microorganisms and clinical settings, e.g., abscesses, bites, blisters, boils, burns, furuncles, lesions, toxin-mediated, ulcers and wounds.
  • Microbial etiologies include:
    • Bacterial: both gram positive and gram negative organisms
    • Fungal, e.g., Candida
    • Mycobacterial, non-tuberculous
    • Parasitic, e.g., Leishmania
    • Viral, e.g., Herpes
