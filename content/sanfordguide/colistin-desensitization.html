Colistin Desensitization

Overview

  • 19-step colistin desensitization protocol.
  • Observe patient during protocol for urticaria, angioedema, shortness of breath, or hemodynamic instability.
  • Reference: Ann Allergy Asthma Immunol 2019;123:607.

Method

  • The quantity of colistin infused is doubled every 15 minutes, starting with 0.00015 mg.
  • Completion of the 4 hour 45 minute protocol results in the cumulative administration of 80 mg colistin.
