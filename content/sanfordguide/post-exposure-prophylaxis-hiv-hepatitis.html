HIV Exposure, Overview

Overview

  • Management of occupational and non-occupational exposure to HIV present in blood, semen, vaginal secretions, other infectious bodily fluids.
  • Free consultation for occupational exposures
    • Call (PEPline) +1-888-448-4911 for expert consultation.
    • Information is also available at clinicalinfo.HIV.gov.
  • General steps in management:
    • Wash clean wounds/flush mucous membranes immediately (use of caustic agents or squeezing the wound is discouraged; data lacking regarding antiseptics).
    • Assess risk by doing the following:
      • Characterize exposure.
      • Determine/evaluate source of exposure by medical history, risk behavior, and testing for HIV.
      • Evaluate and test exposed individual for HIV.
