Nystatin
Tradename: Nystatin
Drug Shortage Information
Prep Started At Notes
Oral suspension 2024-06-21

Usage and Dosing

  • Nystatin oral suspension is an option for mild oropharyngeal candidiasis (OPC).
  • Cream or ointment is an option for paronychia, cutaneous candidiasis.
  • Vaginal tablets are less effective for vulvovaginal candidiasis than other drug choices.

Adult Dose

  • OPC: 500,000 units (5 mL) swish & swallow qid (retain in mouth for 3-5 minutes before swallowing)

Pediatric Dose

Dose (Age >28 Days) Adolescents, children: 500,000 units (5 mL) q6h
Infants: 200,000 units (2 mL) q6h
Premature infants: 100,000 units (1 mL) q6h

Adverse Effects

  • Oral formulations: GI (nausea/vomiting/diarrhea)
  • Topical formulations: rash, skin irritation

Antimicrobial Spectrum

  • C. albicans
  • Not active against Trichophyton, Epidermophyton, or Microsporum (dermatophytes)
