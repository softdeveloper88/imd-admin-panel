Overlapping Toxicities: ARV Drugs & Other Drugs

Introduction

  • The following is a summary of overlapping toxicities between antiretroviral agents (ARVs) and other drugs (both anti-infective and other) commonly used in HIV patients.
  • See also, individual ARV drug pages for drug-drug interactions.
  • See also, ARV Drugs: Adverse Effects by Clinical Presentation.
  • Reference: Guidelines for use of antiretroviral agents in HIV-1 infected adults and adolescents, DHHS, clinicalinfo.HIV.gov.
