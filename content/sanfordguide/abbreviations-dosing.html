Dosing and Other Abbreviations

AD = after dialysis

bid = 2x per day

BSA = body surface area

BW = body weight

CAPD = continuous ambulatory peritoneal dialysis

CDC = U.S. Centers for Disease Control

CrCl = creatinine clearance

CrCln = CrCl normalized for BSA

CRRT = continuous renal replacement therapy

DHHS = U.S. Department of Health & Human Services

div = divided

DR = delayed release

DS = double strength

ECMO = extracorporeal membrane oxygenation

eGFR = estimated glomerular filtration rate

EMA = European Medicines Agency

ER = extended release

FDA = U.S. Food & Drug Administration

HEMO = hemodialysis

IM = intramuscular

IP = intraperitoneal

IT = intrathecal dosing

IV = intravenous dosing

NIH = U.S. National Institutes of Health

PAHO = Pan American Health Organization

po = oral dosing

qAM = in morning

qhs = at hour of sleep

qid = 4x per day

qPM = in evening

qweek = once weekly

q4h = every 4 hours

q6h = every 6 hours

q8h = every 8 hours

q12h = every 12 hours

q24h = every 24 hours

sc = subcutaneous dosing

SLED = sustained low efficiency dialysis

tid = 3x per day

VE = vaccine efficacy

WHO = World Health Organization
