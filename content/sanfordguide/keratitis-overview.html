Keratitis, Overview

Overview

  • Keratitis, infection of the cornea, is usually serious and often sight-threatening.
  • Prompt ophthalmologic consultation is essential!
  • H. simplex virus is the most common etiology of corneal infections in developed countries; bacterial and fungal infections are more common in developing countries.

Specific Topics

  • Viral keratitis
  • Bacterial keratitis
  • Fungal keratitis
  • Protozoan keratitis
  • Mycobacterial keratitis
