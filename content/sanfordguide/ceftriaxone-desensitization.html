Ceftriaxone Desensitization

Overview

  • For desensitization in Ceftriaxone-allergic patients.
  • See Allergol Immunopathol (Madr) 37:105, 2009.

Method

  • Infuse Ceftriaxone IV at 20 minute intervals between doses, as shown in the table.
  • After day 4, give usual dose (1 gm q24h)
