Netilmicin

Usage and Dosing

  • Netilmicin is an aminoglycoside active against aerobic gram-negative bacilli.
  • Potential for cochlear, vestibular and nephrotoxicity.
  • Netilmicin is not available in the U.S.

      Adverse Effects

      • Netilmicin, like all aminoglycosides, is associated with three major groups of adverse effects. See Aminoglycosides, Overview for details.
        • Nephrotoxicity (acute tubular necrosis)
        • Ototoxicity (cochlear and vestibular).
        • Enhancement of neuromuscular blockade (rare).

       

      Pregnancy Risk

      FDA Risk Category 1 Associated with fetal harm, especially 1st TM use. Avoid in pregnancy, although short-term use may be acceptable if benefit outweighs risk.
      Use during Lactation Safety not established, avoid use
