Loracarbef

Usage and Dosing

  • A carbacephem antibiotic; Loracarbef activity is similar to Cefaclor.
  • The drug is no longer available in the U.S., but may be available in other regions.
  • There are no generic versions produced

Adult Dose

Usual Dose200 mg once daily to 400 mg po twice daily

 

Pediatric Dose

Dose (Age >28 Days) 15-30 mg/kg/day (divided q12h)
Max/Day 800 mg

Renal Adjustment

  • Body weight and Creatinine Clearance calculations

  • CAPD = Continuous Ambulatory Peritoneal Dialysis
  • CRRT = Continuous Renal Replacement Therapy
  • AD = after hemodialysis
  • SLED = sustained low efficiency dialysis
