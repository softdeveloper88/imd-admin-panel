Trichuris trichiura

Clinical Setting

  • Whipworm infection
  • Fecal-oral transmission
  • Usually asymptomatic; nonspecific abdominal symptoms with heavy infection
  • Occasional rectal prolapse

Diagnosis

  • Stool exam for ova
  • Adults barely visible to naked eye

Etiologies

  • Trichuris trichiura

Primary Regimens

  • Mebendazole 100 mg po bid x 3 days (cure rate ~70%)
    • Pediatrics:
      • WHO allows Mebendazole use in children 1 year of age and older.
        • CDC states children aged > 2 years limited data
    • Pregnancy:
      • WHO allows use of Mebendazole in 2nd and 3rd trimesters. FDA pregnancy risk category C.
      • It is not known whether mebendazole is excreted in human milk.

Alternative Regimens

  • Albendazole 400 mg po qd x 3 days
    • Albendazole should be used with caution in breastfeeding women.
  • Ivermectin 200 μg/kg po qd x 3 days
  • If poor responses use combination of Ivermectin and mebendazole in doses as above (Lancet Infect Dis 2022; 22:123) 

Antimicrobial Stewardship

  • Unlike other common stool helminths Mebendazole clearly superior to Albendazole for Trichuris (PLoS One. 6:e25003, 2011; N Engl J Med 370:610, 2014)
    • Use Albendazole if co-infection with hookworm has not been ruled out.
    • Recent data on efficacy of single-dose 500 mg chewable pediatric tablet as used in mass treatment campaigns against soil-transmitted helminths Am J Trop Med Hyg 97:1851, 2017.
  • Single dose therapy of either Albendazole or Mebendazole as used in public health programs has lower cure rates and is not acceptable for Trichuris.

Comments

  • Formulation & availability
    • Vermox brand Mebendazole 500 mg tablets is FDA-approved but for donation only and not sold in the U.S.
    • Albendazole 200 mg tablets available from Expert Compounding Pharmacy (see Antiparasitic Drugs, Sources).  
    • Branded albendazole (Albenza) and Mebendazole (Enverm) marketed by Amneal are very costly and is difficult for retail pharmacies to access.  
  • Pyrantel pamoate safe in pregnancy, but no effect against trichiuris or hookworm.
  • Combination therapy with a second agent for single dose public health programs; Albendazole plus Oxantel pamoate (not available in the US) most effective combination, Lancet Infect Dis. 15:277, 2015.
  • Review on Oxantel pamoate: Drugs 81:907, 2021.
  • Emodepside is promising experimental drug with excellent hookworm effect in Tanzania.  N Engl J Med 2023;388:1863.
  • Adding either moxidectin (better) or ivermectin to Albendazole improves egg reduction rate for Trichuris from ~85% to 96-99%.
