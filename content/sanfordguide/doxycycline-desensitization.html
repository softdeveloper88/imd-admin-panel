Doxycycline Desensitization

Overview

  • Protocol for parenteral desensitization to Doxycycline.
  • Observe patient for urticaria, angioedema, shortness of breath, or hemodynamic instability.
  • References: IDCases 11:70, 2018; Ann Allergy Asthma Immunol 111:73, 2013.

Method

  • Doses are administered intravenous push.
  • Completion of the IV protocol results in the cumulative administration of 98.888 mg Doxycycline.
  • 30 minutes after completion, administer Doxycycline 100 mg po and then continue 100 mg po q12h.
