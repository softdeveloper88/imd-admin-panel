Pneumonia, Melioidosis

Clinical Setting

  • Pneumonia with or without septic shock or deep tissue abscess formation (Clin Infect Dis. 2012; 54:362).
  • Endemic regions: tropical climates including Southeast Asia, Northern Australia, South Pacific, Africa, India, China, South America, the Caribbean (Puerto Rico: Clin Infect Dis. 2015; 60:243).
  • Greatest number of cases reported from Thailand.

Etiologies

  • Burkholderia pseudomallei 

Primary Regimens

  • See  Burkholderia pseudomallei

Alternative Regimens

  • None

Comments

  • Reviews: Aust J Gen Pract. 2019; 48:327, Clin Microbiol Rev. 2020;33:e00006, Curr Opin Infect Dis. 2022; 35:517.
  • Even with compliance, relapse rate is 10%; TMP-SMX with lowest relapse rate.
