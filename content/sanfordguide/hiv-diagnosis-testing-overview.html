HIV Diagnosis & Testing, Overview

Overview

  • Overview of topics on diagnosis of HIV-1, HIV-2 and Primary (Acute) HIV infection.
  • For current CDC testing guidance see https://stacks.cdc.gov/view/cdc/23447
  • Opt-Out Testing:  Pts should be told verbally that they will be tested as part of their medical care and given the opportunity to opt out of testing. This discussion should include an explanation about HIV infection, how it is transmitted, and implications about a positive test. If pt declines,

    it should be recorded on his/her chart.
