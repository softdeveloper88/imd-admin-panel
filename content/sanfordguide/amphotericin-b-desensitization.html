Amphotericin B Desensitization

Overview

  • 7-step intravenous desensitization protocol for Liposomal Amphotericin B allergy.
  • Reference: J Allergy Clin Immunol Pract 2017;5:181.

Method

  • This protocol was used in a 17-yo male with ALL requiring a daily dose of approximately 436 mg.
  • General scheme: initial dose of one-millionth of final target dose, then escalating (10-fold) doses administered two hours apart.
  • Premeds used: weight-appropriate doses of hydrocortisone and promethazine, 30 minutes before start of protocol.
  • Time required to complete desensitization: 14 hours.
