Campylobacter gracilis

Clinical Setting

  • Positive lab result
  • Oral flora, associated with periodontal, pleuropulmonary disease and, rarely, bacteremia (Emerging Infect Dis 2015 Jun;21(6):1084-5)

Classification

  • Anaerobic gram-negative rod
  • Formerly Bacteroides gracilis

Primary Regimens

  • Optimal treatment unknown; treat as for Bacteroides fragilis

Alternative Regimens

  • None
