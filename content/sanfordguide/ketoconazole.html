Ketoconazole
Tradename: Nizoral

Usage and Dosing

  • Gastric acid required for absorption of Ketoconazole—cimetidine, omeprazole, antacids block absorption. In achlorhydria, dissolve tablet in 4 mL 0.2N HCl, drink with a straw. Coca-Cola increases absorption by 65%.
  • Potential fatal hepatotoxicity.
  • Little usage of Ketoconazole as less toxic and more potent azoles are available.
  • Caution: numerous drug interactions (see chart below, not all-inclusive)

Adverse Effects

  • Liver toxicity of hepatocellular type reported in about 1:10,000 exposed patients—usually after several days to weeks of exposure.
  • At doses of ≥800 mg/day serum testosterone and plasma cortisol levels fall. With high doses, adrenal (Addisonian) crisis reported.
