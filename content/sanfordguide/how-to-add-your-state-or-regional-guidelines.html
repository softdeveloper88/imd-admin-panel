Information About Local Guidelines

In addition to offering hospital-specific guidelines via Sanford Guide with Stewardship Assist, we are pleased to now offer regional antimicrobial guidelines prepared by national, state and local health departments. New localities will be added over time.

Representatives of national, state, and local health departments are encouraged to reach out to Sanford Guide using this form. We look forward to expanding the scope and features of this new service over the coming months and years.
