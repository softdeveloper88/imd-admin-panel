Genotype Resistance Mutations: NNRTI

  • For non-nucleoside reverse transcriptase inhibitors (NNRTIs), cross-resistance is the rule. "Second generation" NNRTIs (e.g., Doravirine) retain antiviral activity in setting of common NNRTI mutations (e.g. K103N).
