Syphilis, Late Latent

Clinical Setting

  • Latent infection (>1 year) or of indeterminate duration, cardiovascular involvement, late benign gumma.
  • Neurosyphilis is considered separately owing to treatment differences.
  • Not sexually transmissible, treatment is to prevent late consequences of infection. 

Etiologies

  • Treponema pallidum

Primary Regimens

  • Benzathine Penicillin G (Bicillin L-A) 2.4 million units IM q week x 3 weeks = 7.2 million units total dose
  • Interval of 10–14 days between doses of benzathine penicillin for latent syphilis might be acceptable, otherwise start over. 
  • Pregnant women who have delays in any therapy dose >9 days between doses should repeat the full course of therapy.

Alternative Regimens

  • There is no published data on efficacy of alternatives.
  • European 2020 Syphilis Guidelines: Procaine penicillin 600 000 units IM daily during 17–21 days, if benzathine is not available
  • Penicillin allergy or parenteral treatment refused:  unclear, can consider Doxycycline 100 mg BID, but evidence is weak and many experts would recommend desensitization.
  • Pregnant women should be desensitized.

Comments

  • Test all patients with syphilis for HIV and all HIV patients for syphilis.
  • Treat HIV patients with syphilis the same as non-HIV patients.
  • Quantitative nontreponemal serologic tests should be repeated at 6, 12, and 24 months. If does not fall 4-fold after 24 months, will need yearly follow-up of titers and clinical status. Consider retreatment with benzathine penicillin G 2.4 million units IM for 3 weeks if follow-up unlikely or initial titer >1:32 without response.
  • If neurologic symptoms or signs are identified, a CSF evaluation is recommended, with the findings guiding management. Routine lumbar puncture in asymptomatic late syphilis is being questioned in the US. Clin Infect Dis 2015 61:S818 and no longer recommended in 2021 CDC STD guidelines MMWR Recomm Rep 70 (RR-4):1 2021. If CSF exam negative, retreatment benzathine penicillin G 2.4 million units IM for 3 weeks.
