Remdesivir Desensitization

Overview

  • 12-step intravenous desensitization protocol for remdesivir allergy.
  • Reference: Ann Pharmacother 2023;57:110.
  • Cumulative drug infused: 100 mg.
  • Time required to complete desensitization: 337.5 minutes (5 hr 37.5 min).

Method

  • Prepare three solutions of remdesivir:
    • Solution 1: 1 mg in D5W 250 mL (0.004 mg/mL)
    • Solution 2: 10 mg in D5W 250 mL (0.04 mg/mL)
    • Solution 3: 100 mg in D5W 250 mL (0.4 mg/mL)
  • Perform the 12-step desensitization as follows:
