Nucleoside / Nucleotide Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors (NRTI)

  • (Black Box warning for all NRTIs: lactic acidosis/hepatic steatosis, potentially fatal.
  • Also carry Warnings that fat redistribution and immune reconstitution syndromes have been observed.)
  • Abacavir
  • Didanosine
  • Emtricitabine
  • Lamivudine
  • Stavudine
  • Tenofovir alafenamide fumarate
  • Tenofovir disproxil fumarate
  • Zidovudine

Non-Nucleoside Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors (NNRTI)

  • (Black Box warning for all NNRTIs: lactic acidosis/hepatic steatosis, potentially fatal. Also carry Warnings that fat redistribution and immune reconstitution syndromes have been observed).
  • Doravirine
  • Efavirenz
  • Etravirine
  • Nevirapine
  • Rilpivirine

Protease inhibitors (PI)

  • Abnormalities in glucose metabolism, dyslipidemias, fat redistribution syndromes are potential problems.
  • Patients taking PI may be at increased risk for developing osteopenia/osteoporosis.
  • Spontaneous bleeding episodes have been reported in HIV+ patients with hemophilia being treated with PI.
  • Rheumatoid complications have been reported with use of PIs (Ann Rheum Dis 61:82, 2002).
  • Potential of some PIs for QTc prolongation has been suggested (Lancet 365:682, 2005).
  • Caution for all PIs—Co-administration with certain drugs dependent on CYP3A for elimination & for which ↑ levels can cause serious toxicity may be contraindicated. As with other classes, treatment may result in immune reconstitution syndromes.
  • Atazanavir
  • Darunavir
  • Fosamprenavir
  • Indinavir
  • Nelfinavir
  • Ritonavir
  • Saquinavir
  • Tipranavir
