Endophthalmitis, Overview

  • Categories include endogenous (seeding from fungemia or bacteremia), post-traumatic, post-intravitreal injection, post-surgical.
  • Loss of vision, pain, photophobia, red eye, haziness of vitreous.
  • Prior to therapy, culture needle aspirate of both vitreous and aqueous humor.
  • Intravitreal administration of antimicrobials is essential.

Specific Topics

  • Hematogenous endophthalmitis
  • Candida endophthalmitis
  • Post-traumatic endophthalmitis
  • Post-filtering blebs (glaucoma)
  • Post-surgical or post-injection endophthalmitis
