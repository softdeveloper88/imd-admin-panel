Gastroenteritis, Diarrhea: Overview

Overview

  • IDSA Guideline: Clin Infect Dis 2017;65:1963.
  • Often the infectious agent will not be known, so if symptoms are severe, empiric treatment will have to be initiated.
  • See American Gastroenterology Society guidelines (Am J Gastroenterol 111:602, 2016) and similar guidelines by International Society of Travel Medicine for travelers' diarrhea (J Travel Med 24:S57, 2017). 
  • See Empiric therapy, Gastroenteritis, Overview for an overview of diagnostic considerations.
