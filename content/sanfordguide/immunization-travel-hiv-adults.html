Immunization, Travel, HIV+ Adults

Introduction

  • Certain immunizations are routinely recommended for HIV+ adults when traveling to developing countries.
  • Other immunizations may be recommended based on itinerary and/or activity.
  • All travelers should have current routine immunizations, see Routine Immunization Recommendations for HIV+ Adults.
  • For further information, see www.fitfortravel.scot.nhs.uk or review about vaccines for international travel Mayo Clin Proc. 94:2314, 2019.
  • COVID-19 (SARS-CoV-2) vaccines are recommended for HIV+ individuals and may be required, depending upon the region.
