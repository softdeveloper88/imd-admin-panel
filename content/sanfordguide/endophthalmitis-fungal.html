Endophthalmitis, Candida

Clinical Setting

  • Endogenous: Ocular involvement occurs in 1.8% of patients with candidemia although in a subgroup analysis of Asian countries rates were 3.6% (Clin Infect Dis 76: 1738, 2023)
    • Chorioretinitis accounts for 85% of ocular disease while endophthalmitis occurs in only 15% (Clin Infect Dis 53:262, 2011).
    • Ocular disease is most often asymptomatic
    • Routine ophthalmology consult to obtain a dilated eye exam in patients with candidemia is recommended (Clin Infect Dis 62:409, 2016) although some debate exists (JAMA Ophthalmol 137; 698, 2019)
  • Exogenous: Candidal infection can occur following ocular surgery or traumatic injury.
  • Consultation with / referral to ophthalmologist recommended 

Etiologies

  • Candida albicans is most common but disease occurs with other Candida sp.

Primary Regimens

  • Chorioretinitis w/o vitritis
    • Fluconazole 800 mg (12 mg/kg) loading dose then 400-800 mg (6-12 mg/kg daily) (poor activity against C. glabrata or C. krusei)
    • C. albicans and other fluconazole/voriconazole susceptible isolates
    • Candida non-susceptible to fluconazole or voriconazole
      • Lipid-based Amphotericin B 3-5 mg/kg daily +/- Flucytosine 25 mg/kg qid
    • Consider adding intravitreal Amphotericin B 0.005–0.01 mg in 0.1 mL sterile water OR Voriconazole 100 μg in 0.1 mL sterile water for sight-threatening disease
  • Chorioretinitis with vitritis (Endophthalmitis)
    • Systemic therapy as above PLUS intravitreal therapy as above PLUS strongly consider vitrectomy

Alternative Regimens

  • None

Antimicrobial Stewardship

  • Duration of therapy: 4-6 weeks or longer, based on repeated retinal exams

Comments

  • Echinocandins not recommended for the treatment of ocular candida infection.
  • Intravitreal amphotericin and/or vitrectomy may be necessary for those with vitritis or endophthalmitis (Br J Ophthalmol 92:466, 2008; Pharmacotherapy 27:1711, 2007).
  • IDSA guidelines: Clin Infect Dis 62:409, 2016 and Clin Infect Dis 53:262, 2011.
