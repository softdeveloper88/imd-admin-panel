Surgical Prophylaxis, Peritoneal Dialysis Catheter

Clinical Setting

  • Antimicrobial prophylaxis for peritoneal dialysis catheter placement procedure.
  • See Prevention of Surgical Infections, Overview.

Etiologies

  • Bacterial

Primary Regimens

  • Vancomycin single 1 gm IV dose 12 hrs prior to procedure

Alternative Regimens

  • None

Comments

  • Prophylaxis reduced peritonitis during 14 days post-placement in 221 patients; infection rates were: Vancomycin 1%, Cefazolin 7%, placebo 12% (p=0.02) (Am J Kidney Dis 36:1014, 2000, Perit Dial Int. 37(2):141, 2017).
  • Related topic: Treatment of CAPD peritonitis.
