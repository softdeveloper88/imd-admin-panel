Imipenem-cilastatin Desensitization

Overview

  • Protocol for rapid intravenous imipenem-cilastatin desensitization, followed by continuous infusion.
  • Observe patient for urticaria, angioedema, shortness of breath, or hemodynamic instability.
  • Reference: Ann Pharmacother 2003;37:513.

Method

  • In this case report, a nine-step protocol delivers 34.3 mg of imipenem-cilastatin over 4.5 hours.
  • This is followed immediately by a continuous infusion at 42 mg/hr (1008 mg infused over 24 hours). This daily dose was selected because of renal impairment in the case patient.
  • Each protocol dose is prepared in a 50 mL normal saline minibag and infused over 30 minutes.
  • Note that reconstituted imipenem-cilastatin is stable for only 4 hours at room temperature (24 hours if refrigerated).
