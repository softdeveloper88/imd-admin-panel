Neomycin

Usage and Dosing

  • Neomycin is an oral aminoglycoside used as bowel preparation pre-operatively for resectional GI surgery and for treatment of hepatic encephalopathy.
  • Systemic absorption can occur and lead to ototoxicity, neurotoxicity or nephrotoxicity (Black Box Warning).
  • Low concentration in non-prescription topical creams and ointments.
  • Active in vitro vs. staphylococci and most aerobic Gram-negative bacteria; resistant to streptococci and Gram-positive bacilli

Adult Dose

Bowel preparation,
pre-GI surgery

1 gm at 1, 2 and 11 pm on day preceding surgery + Erythromycin 1 gm po at the same times + mechanical cleansing
Hepatic encephalopathy500-2000 mg q6-8h for 5-6 days; seek safer alternative for longer use

Comments
