Vaccines, Overview

Overview

  • This page divides Sanford Guide vaccine coverage into adult and pediatric travel vaccines, routine adult and pediatric vaccines with travel-related indications, travel-related updating and considerations in special populations.
  • Routine pediatric vaccination is not covered.
  • Coverage is based on U.S. approved vaccines and recommendations with occasional comments on WHO approaches and vaccines widely available outside the US.
  • Vaccines of seasonal or particular interest in 2024:
    • COVID-19 (Age ≥5 yrs)(mRNA)
    • COVID-19 (Age <5 yrs)(mRNA)
    • Novavax COVID-19
    • RSV
    • Chikungunya
  • Additional resources:
    • Comprehensive reviews of vaccines for travel: Mayo Clin Proc. 2019, 94:2314;  N Engl J Med 2016, 375:247.
  • Note: Expanded adult travel vaccine coverage (shown below) may require an additional subscription.
