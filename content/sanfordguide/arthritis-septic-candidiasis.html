Arthritis, Septic, Candida

Clinical Setting

  • Synovial fluid culture positive for Candida sp.

Etiologies

  • C. albicans
  • Other Candida sp.

Primary Regimens

  • Fluconazole 400 mg (6 mg/kg) IV/po qd x 6 weeks  (for susceptible species)
  • Caspofungin, 70 mg IV loading dose then 50 mg daily
  • Micafungin 100 mg IV daily
  • Anidulafungin 200 mg IV loading dose then 100 mg daily

Alternative Regimens

  • Lipid-based Amphotericin B 3–5 mg/kg IV qd

Comments

  • Surgical debridement is indicated in all cases; removal of prosthetic joints whenever possible (Scand J Infect Dis 42:890, 2010); treat for at least 6-8 weeks and indefinitely if retained hardware.
  • Osteomyelitis should be treated for up to 6 months. See IDSA treatment guidelines:  Clin Infect Dis 62:409, 2016.
