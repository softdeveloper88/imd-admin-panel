HIV Combined Antigen Antibody Tests & Home Tests

Combined Antigen Antibody Tests

  • Combined HIV antigen-antibody tests detect both HIV antibody and HIV p24 antigen. Hence, detects p24 antigen in window prior to antibody formation.
  • Can also detect acute (or primary) HIV infection.
  • Multiple tests are available from several manufacturers.
  • Combination Antigen / Antibody Tests
    • Detects HIV-1 and HIV-2 antibody, both adults and children (age ≥ 2 years).
    • Approved for pediatric use and in pregnancy.
    • Can use serum or plasma.
    • Tests (Manufacturers)
      • Architect (Abbott)
      • GS HIV Combo (Biorad)
      • Determine (Alere) 
      • Advia Centaur (Siemens)
      • Virtros (Ortho)
