Yellow Fever Vaccine Desensitization

Overview

  • If yellow fever immunization is imperative, and the individual has a history of severe egg sensitivity and has a positive skin test to the vaccine (YF-Vax), this 5-step procedure may be used.
  • Be sure the necessary emergency equipment for management of anaphylaxis is immediately available.
  • Reference: manufacturer's product labeling.

Method

  • Administer the following successive doses subcutaneously at 15-20 minute intervals:
