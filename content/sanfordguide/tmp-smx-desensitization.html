TMP-SMX Desensitization

Overview

  • Desensitization of patients who are allergic to Trimethoprim-Sulfamethoxazole (TMP-SMX).
  • Reference: Clin Infect Dis 20:849, 1995.

Method

  • Perform in hospital or clinic.
    • Use oral suspension [40 mg TMP + 200 mg SMX per 5 mL].
    • Take 6 ounces of water after each dose.
    • Corticosteroids, antihistamines are NOT used.
