Ceftazidime Desensitization

Overview

  • 12-step rapid intravenous desensitization protocol for ceftazidime allergy.
  • References: Curr Opin Allergy Clin Immunol 2006;6:476.

Method

  • Cumulative drug infused: 1000 mg.
  • Time required to complete desensitization: 349 min (about 5.8 hours).
  • Prepare three solutions:
    • Solution 1: 10 mg in D5W 250 mL (0.04 mg/mL)
    • Solution 2: 100 mg in D5W 250 mL (0.4 mg/mL)
    • Solution 3: 1000 mg in D5W 250 mL (4 mg/mL)
