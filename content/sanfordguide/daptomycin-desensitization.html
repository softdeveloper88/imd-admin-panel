Daptomycin Desensitization

Overview

  • The following method is used to desensitize patients with Daptomycin allergy.
  • Observe patient for urticaria, angioedema, shortness of breath, or hemodynamic instability.
  • Reference: Ann Allergy Asthma Immunol 100:87, 2008.

Method

  • Infuse each dose over 15 minutes
