Haemophilus parainfluenzae

Clinical Setting

  • H. parainfluenzae causes a variety of infections including periodontal infections, otitis media, sinusitis, pneumonia, osteomyelitis, bacteremia, and endocarditis (one the the HACEK organisms).
  • Found in normal oral flora.
  • Susceptibilities similar to H. influenzae and may produce beta-lactamase.

Etiologies

  • Gram-negative coccobacillus

Primary Regimens

  • See H. influenzae and HACEK endocarditis

Alternative Regimens

  • See H. influenzae and HACEK endocarditis
