Causes of hemoptysis

Pulmonary

  • Bronchitis
  • Lung cancer
  • Bronchiectasis

Cardiac

  • Mitral stenosis/acute pulmonary edema

Infectious

  • Tuberculosis
  • Lung abscess
  • Bacterial pneumonia
  • Aspergillosis

Hematologic

  • Coagulopathy

Vascular

  • Pulmonary embolism
  • Arteriovenous malformation

Systemic disease

  • Granulomatosis with polyangiitis
  • Goodpasture syndrome

Other
  • Trauma
  • Cocaine use (inhalation)
