Guidelines for lithium therapy

Indications
  • Acute mania, bipolar maintenance

Contraindications
  • Chronic kidney disease
  • Heart disease
  • Hyponatremia or diuretic use

Baseline studies
  • Blood urea nitrogen, creatinine, calcium, urinalysis
  • Thyroid function tests
  • ECG in patients with coronary risk factors

Adverse

effects

Acute

  • Tremor, ataxia, weakness
  • Polyuria, polydipsia
  • Vomiting, diarrhea
  • Cognitive impairment

Chronic

  • Nephrogenic diabetes insipidus
  • Chronic kidney disease
  • Thyroid dysfunction
  • Hyperparathyroidism
