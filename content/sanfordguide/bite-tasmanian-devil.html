Bite: Tasmanian Devil

Clinical Setting

  • Bite would from Tasmanian devil.

Etiologies

  • Pasteurella multocida (Clin Infect Dis 14:1266, 1992)

Primary Regimens

  • Treat as for Cat bite.

Alternative Regimens

  • None

Comments

  • 15% of P. multocida from devils were TMP-SMX resistant (Lett Appl Microbiol 62: 237, 2016)
  • Administer tetatus toxoid/Tdap if > 10 years since last dose
  • See: Clin Infect Dis 2014;59(2):e10–52
