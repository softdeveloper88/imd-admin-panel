Otitis Externa, Chronic

Clinical Setting

  • Chronic inflammatory condition of the skin of the external ear canal.
  • Results in edema and hypertrophy.

Etiologies

  • Unknown

Primary Regimens

  • Remove irritants (Q-tips, water, soaps, shampoos, etc.)
  • Triamcinolone 0.1% to effected area

Alternative Regimens

  • Evaluate for otomycosis, if present treat as such

Comments

  • Review of chronic otitis externa: Curr Opin Otolaryngol Head Neck Surg 19:341, 2011.
