Entecavir Desensitization

Overview

  • 11-step oral entecavir desensitization protocol.
  • Observe patient during protocol for urticaria, angioedema, shortness of breath, or hemodynamic instability.
  • Reference: Ann Allergy Asthma Immunol 2019;123:312.

Method

  • Stock solution prepared by crushing a 0.5 mg tab and adding Ora-Plus (to a stock concentration of 0.05 mg/mL), with further dilution for steps 1-6.
  • Completion of the 2 hour 45 minute protocol results in the cumulative administration of 0.5 mg entecavir.
  • Doses are administered in 15 minute intervals.
