Arthritis, Joint Infections, Overview

Overview

  • Arthritis, bursitis, prosthetic joint infections
  • Septic arthritis: Treatment requires both adequate drainage of purulent joint fluid and appropriate antimicrobial therapy. There is no need to inject antimicrobials into joints. Empiric therapy after collection of blood and joint fluid for culture; review Gram stain of joint fluid.
