Mycobacterium gordonae

Clinical Setting

  • Common contaminant.
  • Rare cause of pulmonary infection in HIV (Int J STD AIDS 20:193, 2009) and lung transplant recipients (Transpl Infect Dis 14:452, 2012) and often a colonizer even in these settings.

Etiologies

  • Mycobacterium gordonae

Primary Regimens

  • Reserve treatment for those with convincing evidence of invasive disease.
  • Regimens are not defined, but consider Rifabutin 300 mg po once daily + Ethambutol 15 mg/kg po once daily + Ciprofloxacin 750 mg po bid.

Alternative Regimens

  • None

Comments

  • See Lancet Infect Dis 2022; 22: e178-90.
  • In vitro sensitivity to Clarithromycin, Linezolid, other fluoroquinolones (Antimicrob Agents Chemother 47:1736, 2003; Am J Respir Crit Care Med 175:367, 2007; Microbiol Spectr 2016 Nov;4(6)).
  • Resistant to Isoniazid, Pyrazinamide; variable activity of Rifampin.
