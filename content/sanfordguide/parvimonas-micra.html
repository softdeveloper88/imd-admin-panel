Parvimonas micra

Clinical Setting

  • Positive lab result
  • Oral and gastrointestinal flora. Associated with odontogenic infections and, rarely, bacteremia, abscess (Anaerobe 2018, 54:136)

Classification

  • Anerobic gram-positive coccus

Primary Regimens

  • Typically susceptible to Penicillin, Clindamycin, Metronidazole

Alternative Regimens

  • A carbapenem

Comments

  • See Eur J Clin Microbiol Infect Dis. 2023;42:903 and Microorganisms. 2021;9:1665 for cases series and antimicrobial susceptibilities
