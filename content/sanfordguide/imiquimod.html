Imiquimod
Tradename: Aldara

Usage and Dosing

  • Imiquimod is available as a 5% topical cream for treatment of warts.
  • Warts: external genital and/or perianal condylomata acuminata only.

Adult Dose

Usual Dose5% cream. Thin layer applied at bedtime, washing off after 6-10 hr, 3x weekly on non-consecutive days to maximum of 16 weeks.

Pediatric Dose

Dose (Age >28 Days) Genital warts (age ≥12 yrs):
Apply 3x/week, max 16 weeks
(wash off after 6-10 hrs)

Adverse Effects

  • Erythema, itching & burning, erosions.
  • Flu-like syndrome
  • Photosensitivity, increased susceptibility to sunburn (avoid UV).
