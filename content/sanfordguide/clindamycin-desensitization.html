Clindamycin Desensitization

Overview

  • Protocol for rapid oral desensitization to clindamycin in a pediatric patient.
  • Observe patient for urticaria, angioedema, shortness of breath, or hemodynamic instability.
  • Reference: J Allergy Clin Immunol Pract 2018;6:2141.

Method

  • Completion of protocol results in the cumulative administration of 300 mg clindamycin.
  • Subsequent 300 mg doses are administered q8h.
  • Consider premedication (e.g. antihistamines) before desensitization and subsequent doses.
