Calculation of Child-Pugh Score

Impaired Hepatic Function

  • Child-Pugh Score is used in determination of dose adjustment in patients with liver disease.
  • See Clin Infect Dis 40:174, 2005.

Calculation of Child-Pugh Score

 

Clinical FeatureScore: 1Score: 2Score: 3
Encephalopathy (see below)NoneGrade 1-2Grade 3-4
Albumin>3.5 gm/dL2.8–3.5 gm/dL<2.8 gm/dL
Total bilirubin*<2 mg/dL2–3 mg/dL>3 mg/dL
Prothrombin time/INR<4/<1.74-6/1.7-2.3>2.3
AscitesAbsentMild-moderateSevere/refractory

 * For patients with Gilbert’s syndrome and patients on antiretroviral drugs that increase indirect bilirubin (e.g., indinavir and atazanavir), the bilirubin should be scored as <2 mg/dL (Ref: expert opinion).
