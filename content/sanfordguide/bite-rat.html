Bite: Rat

Clinical Setting

  • Bite wound from rat, rodent.
  • Rat bite fever is caused by Spirillum minus (Asia) and Streptobacillus moniliformis (USA).

Etiologies

  • Spirillum minus
  • Streptobacillus moniliformis

Primary Regimens

  • Prophylaxis: Amoxicillin-clavulanate 875/125 mg po bid x 3 days
  • Rat bite fever: Penicillin G 2 million units IV q4h or Doxycycline 100 mg po bid x 10-14 days, depending on clinical response

Alternative Regimens

  • Prophylaxis: Doxycycline 100 mg po bid x 3 days
  • Rat bite fever: Ceftriaxone 2 gm IV q24h x 10-14 days, depending on clinical response

Comments

  • Rabies post-exposure prophylaxis and vaccination is not indicated for rat bites.
