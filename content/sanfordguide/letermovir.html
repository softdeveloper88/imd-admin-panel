Letermovir

Usage and Dosing

  • Indicated for prophylaxis of cytomegalovirus (CMV) infection and disease in seropositive recipients of an allogeneic hematopoietic stem cell transplant (N Engl J Med 2017; 377:2433).
  • Letermovir is an inhibitor of the CMV terminase complex. The main function of the terminase complex is to cleave the long DNA chain produced during CMV replication (which contains multiple repeated gene sequences) into single units of functional CMV DNA.
  • Letermovir has no activity against other herpesviruses.
  • Because of its novel mechanism of action, there should be little cross-resistance with currently available anti-CMV drugs.
  • Letermovir is not approved for treatment for CMV infection.
  • Caution: numerous drug interactions (see Major Interactions chart below, not all-inclusive).

Adult Dose

  • 480 mg IV or po q24h
  • Infuse IV doses over one hour, not as bolus injection

  • Cough
  • Headache
  • Peripheral edema
