Canaliculitis

Clinical Setting

  • Infection of lacrimal apparatus
  • Digital pressure produces exudate at punctum.
  • Gram stain confirms diagnosis.

Etiologies

  • Staphylococci
  • Streptococci
  • Actinomyces sp.
  • Arachnia, Fusobacterium, Nocardia sp., Candida sp., rapid growing mycobacteria (all rare)

Primary Regimens

  • Apply hot packs to punctal area 4x/day
  • Referral to ophthalmologist

Alternative Regimens

  • None

Comments

  • Surgical therapy may be required.
