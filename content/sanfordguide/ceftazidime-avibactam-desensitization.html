Ceftazidime-avibactam Desensitization

Overview

  • 12-step Ceftazidime-avibactam desensitization protocol.
  • Premedicate with diphenhydramine 50 mg IV plus H2RA IV.
  • Observe patient during protocol for urticaria, angioedema, shortness of breath, or hemodynamic instability.
  • Reference: Fed Pract 2022;39:94.

Method

  • Three solutions are required: 0.5 mg/mL, 5 mg/mL, and 50 mg/mL.
  • After step 12, administer target dose of 2.5 gm IV q8h.
