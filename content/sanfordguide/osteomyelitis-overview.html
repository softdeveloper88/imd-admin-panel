Osteomyelitis, Overview

Overview

  • Microbiologic diagnosis of osteomyelitis is essential.
  • If blood culture is negative, then culture of bone is needed.
  • Culture of sinus drainage not recommended as it is not predictive of bone culture.
  • Cure rates are improved by surgical debridement of dead bone and drainage of associated abscesses.
  • For a comprehensive review of antimicrobial penetration into bone: Clinical Pharmacokinetics 48:89, 2009 and J Clin Pharm Ther 38:89, 2013.
  • Review: principles of therapy and use of oral agents: Infect Dis Clin North Am. 2025 Apr 11:S0891-5520(25)00014
