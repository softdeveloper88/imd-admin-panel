Technivie: Ombitasvir + Paritaprevir + Ritonavir
Tradename: Technivie: Ombitasvir + Paritaprevir + Ritonavir

Usage and Dosing

  • NOTE: THIS PRODUCT WAS DISCONTINUED IN 2019 AND IS NO LONGER AVAILABLE.
  • Technivie is a fixed-dose combination of Ombitasvir + paritaprevir / ritonavir indicated in combination with Ribavirin for treatment of genotype 4 Hepatitis C virus (HCV) in patients without cirrhosis
  • Caution: numerous drug interactions and contraindications (see Table below, not all-inclusive)

Adult Dose

  • Two tablets po once daily in the AM with food + Ribavirin x 12 weeks. One tablet of Technivie contains Ombitasvir 12.5 mg, Paritaprevir 75 mg, and Ritonavir 50 mg.

Pediatric Dose

Dose (Age >28 Days) Safety and efficacy not established
(not available in US)
Max/Day -

Renal Adjustment

  • None

Hepatic Adjustment

Mild impairment (Child-Pugh Class A) No dosage adjustment
Moderate impairment (Child-Pugh Class B) Contraindicated
Severe impairment (Child-Pugh Class C) Contraindicated

Adverse Effects

  • Contraindications: severe hepatic impairment; co-administration with drugs that are highly dependent on CYP3A for clearance; known hypersensitivity reaction to Ritonavir, e.g., Stevens-Johnson syndrome.
  • Most common: asthenia, fatigue, nausea, insomnia
  • Black Box Warning: Cases of HBV reactivation, occasionally fulminant, during or after DAA therapy have been reported in HBV/HCV coninfected patients who were not already on HBV suppressive therapy

Antimicrobial Spectrum

  • Hepatitis C virus (HCV), genotype 4

Pregnancy Risk

FDA Risk Category 1 B
Use during Lactation Safety not established, avoid use if possible
