Overview

  • Neisseria gonorrheae

  • Prostatitis, acute
  • Prostatitis, chronic

  • Vaginitis, bacterial

Fungal

  • Candidiasis

    • Disseminated
    • Meningitis
    • Pulmonary
  • Cryptococcosis
  • Histoplasmosis
  • Penicilliosis

  • Sporotrichosis

Parasitic

  • Cryptosporidium parvum, cryptosporidium hominis
  • Entamoeba histolytica, amebiasis
  • Isosporiasis, cystoisopora belli
  • Microsporidiosis

  • Strongyloidiasis, strongyloides stercoralis
  • Ectoparasites (lice): body, head, pubic
  • Scabies

Viral

  • Cytomegalovirus (CMV)
  • Epstein Barr Virus (EBV), Hairy leukoplakia
  • Herpes simplex virus (HSV)
  • Human herpes 8 virus
  • Human papillomavirus
  • Progressive multifocal leukoencephalopathy (PML)
  • Varicella zoster virus (VZV)

Other Conditions
